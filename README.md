1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Web Presence Agency is a CS/IT hybrid digital agency targeting small-to-medium businesses that lack a professional online presence. Founded by an IT Networking specialist and a Computer Science backend developer, the agency delivers not just websites but complete digital infrastructure: secure hosting, AI-powered automation, SEO performance, and ongoing technical support.
Version 2.0 of this plan introduces revised pricing that reflects true market value, a fourth AI-powered service tier, and a systematized sales engine built around a free business audit. The agency is designed to generate recurring monthly revenue from day one.

5. OPERATIONAL ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES
IT Lead (IT Networking Partner): Primary Focus: Client-Facing / Infrastructure. Domain/DNS, hosting environments, server security, client onboarding, AI tool configuration, sales, and client relationships.
CS Lead (CS / Backend Partner): Primary Focus: Build / Product. Frontend design, UI/UX, custom scripts, API integrations, SEO performance, backend systems, AI workflow builds.
Both: Know everything about the product and infrastructure.
Operational Rules:
• One person owns client relationships (IT Lead). One person owns the build (CS Lead).
• Equity split must be documented in a signed operating agreement.
• Set a revenue milestone ($2,000/month) before either partner draws a salary.
6. SALES & MARKETING ENGINE
The Free Audit — Primary Sales Tool
The target customer is NOT searching online for web services; they don’t know they have a problem. The sales engine must bring the problem to them, visually and in person. 
Walk into any local business with a one-page printed audit showing their current Google ranking, their top competitor's ranking, estimated monthly leads being lost, and estimated revenue impact. This converts without a sales pitch because it quantifies a pain they already feel.
